Gerry Straathof is completing a Masters in Fine Arts. The body of work created within the context of this degree is about early homesteaders,vanished villages and industries of Western Canada. This has involved creating ways to record the process of exploration within a site, and creating artistic methods for demonstrating the personal feeling of being in abandoned spaces where people lived and worked.
